Our Story

FoodBites was founded by a group of friends in the early 2010s from New York city. The main goal of these friends was to be able to share their love for bite sized portions of any food imaginable that could be easily shared with your loved ones.

You can expect to be greeted with the aroma of various saviory,sweet,or salty Bite sized treats. You may also expect to greeted with open arms no matter your taste preference. Talk about the food, environment, or customer experience.
